# Welcome to Physical AI

Welcome to the **Physical AI Book** - your comprehensive guide to understanding the fascinating world of robotics, embodied artificial intelligence, and intelligent physical systems.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that interact with and operate in the physical world through robotic bodies, sensors, and actuators. Unlike traditional AI that exists purely in software, Physical AI must:

- **Perceive** the physical world through sensors
- **Reason** about spatial relationships and physical constraints
- **Act** on the environment through motors and actuators
- **Learn** from physical interactions and experiences
